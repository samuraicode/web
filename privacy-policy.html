IsItOut Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 19, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At IsItOut, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your information when you use our movie release tracking service.

Information We Do NOT Collect

IsItOut does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports containing personal data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

How IsItOut Works

IsItOut operates entirely on your device:

  • Local Storage: Your movie watchlist and preferences are stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • Movie Data: We fetch movie information from The Movie Database (TMDB) API to provide you with accurate release dates, posters, and movie details
  • No Account Required: The app does not require registration or account creation
  • No Data Transmission: Your personal movie lists and preferences never leave your device

Third-Party Services

IsItOut uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie information. When you search for movies:

  • Search queries are sent to TMDB to retrieve movie data
  • These queries do not contain any personal information
  • TMDB's privacy policy governs their data handling practices
  • You can review TMDB's privacy policy at: https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

Data Security

Since IsItOut does not collect personal information:

  • There is no personal data at risk of breach
  • Your movie watchlist remains private on your device
  • All app data is protected by your device's built-in security features

Children's Privacy

IsItOut is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Since we do not collect contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates.

App Store Privacy Labels

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, IsItOut's privacy label indicates:

  • Data Not Collected: This app does not collect any data
  • Data Not Linked to You: No data is linked to your identity
  • Data Not Used to Track You: No data is used for tracking
